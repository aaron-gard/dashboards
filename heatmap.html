💡 Strategic Implications

⚠️ Myer Dependency — Existential Concentration Risk

90% of HP’s physical footprint is inside Myer. Greatest strength (premium positioning, low overhead, national reach) and greatest risk. If Myer contracts or restructures concessions, HP loses presence at scale. The 6 standalone stores are the only HP-controlled retail. Diversifying toward standalone locations is a medium-term priority.

📋 Flat AOV = Need-Based Buying Pattern

AOV varies <$4 across all distances ($131.91–$135.91). Textbook need-based purchasing: comfort footwear buyers spend the same whether near Myer or 100km away. Driven by workplace requirements, health needs, and age-related comfort. Every new catchment converts at identical economics.
