🔥 The Halo Effect: Stores Drive Online Demand

The central finding of this analysis: physical stores lift online demand in surrounding postcodes by 2–5x. This holds across every brand with physical presence, regardless of category, price point, or customer demographic. Postcodes within 10km of a store consistently generate more online revenue per capita than those without coverage.

What This Means for Store P&Ls

A store doesn’t just generate in-store revenue — it lifts online sales in a 10–20km radius. Store business cases that only measure four-wall performance are fundamentally undervaluing the asset. The online halo should be included in every store opening, renewal, and closure decision.

Format Matters More Than Count

JM’s 221 stockists generate a 77.1% halo. Champion’s 6 DFO outlets generate 19.4%. The difference isn’t just volume — outlet-format stores attract bargain-driven customers who don’t convert to full-price online at the same rate. A full-price store produces 3–5x the online halo of an outlet.

📈 Methodology

Data sources: Shopify order data (CY2025 via Polar Analytics, extracted Feb 15 2026), ABS Census 2021, store locations from Roseperl CDN. Halo analysis: Each postcode’s demand mapped to distance from nearest JM stockist (221 locations including 56 Myer, 164 independent, 1 Shoe Warehouse) using haversine formula. State breakdown: Orders and demand aggregated by customer shipping state. AOV analysis: Calculated as total demand / total orders per state. Stockist efficiency: DTC demand per stockist by state (note: this measures online demand near stores, not in-store sales). Myer data: Wholesale revenue ($720K FY YTD) provided by Brand Collective commercial team; not included in Polar Analytics DTC figures. Competitive mapping: Store counts and AOV estimates based on public data and industry knowledge. Expansion scoring: Demand density (40%) + male 25–55 demographic (25%) + competitive white space (20%) + household income (15%). Limitations: ABS data is 2021 Census estimates; Polar Analytics captures DTC only (max 1,000 postcode rows); Myer wholesale is FY YTD not CY-aligned; stockist data from Roseperl CDN may lag actual network changes.
