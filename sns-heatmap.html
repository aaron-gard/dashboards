💰 AOV: Need-Based, Not Distance-Driven

💡 Strategic Insights

1. Dual-channel network is a strength. 40 own stores + 56 Myer concessions gives a healthier partner dependency than brands relying solely on Myer. Brand experience isn't hostage to a single wholesale partner.

2. Physical presence drives frequency. The flat AOV but strong halo effect means stores drive repeat visits, not bigger baskets. Parents near stores come back every season. Every new location unlocks this flywheel.

3. Remote growth = unserved demand. +28% growth in the 50-100km band isn't organic. It signals markets where digital marketing is working but no store exists to convert the brand awareness into habitual purchasing.

4. Portfolio YoY: +16%. $15,061,824 in CY2025 vs $13,030,940 in CY2024. Growth is broad-based across distance bands, not just near-store cannibalisation of foot traffic.
